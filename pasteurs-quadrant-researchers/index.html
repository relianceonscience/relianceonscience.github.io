Pasteur's Quadrant Researchers

download data > documentation

Boundary-spanning scientists (1976–2023) who both publish and patent — we capture these boundary-spanners in a public database of Pasteur's Quadrant Researchers (PQRs). To identify individuals who both publish science and patent inventions, we linked a database of disambiguated scientists (OpenAlex) to disambiguated inventors (PatentsView). OpenAlex covers most scientific papers published worldwide since 1800; PatentsView relies on the USPTO, which covers inventions registered in the United States since 1976 (though roughly half originate outside the U.S., giving some international coverage of the typically more important inventions). We rely on the underlying disambiguations of OpenAlex authors and PatentsView inventors.

Files

The dataset is pqrs_dataset.tsv. Two linkage files are also provided: authorid_workid.tsv (OpenAlex author–work links) and inventorid_patentid.tsv (PatentsView inventor–patent links). A data description accompanies them. All files are hosted at Zenodo (DOI 10.5281/zenodo.11374125).
