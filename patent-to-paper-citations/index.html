Patent-to-Paper Citations

Front-page and in-text citations from patents to scientific articles published since 1800. Each citation carries an applicant/examiner flag, a confidence score (1–10), and where it was found (front page only, body text only, or both). We link to papers captured by OpenAlex.

Updated July 2026: coverage now extends through patents granted in 2025, with the previously missing 2021–2022 in-text citations restored. USPTO patents, confidence ≥ 4.

Citing the data

If you use these data, please cite both:

Marx, M. & A. Fuegi (2020). Reliance on Science: Worldwide Front-Page Patent Citations to Scientific Articles. Strategic Management Journal 41(9): 1572–1594. doi:10.1002/smj.3145
Marx, M. & A. Fuegi (2022). Reliance on Science by Inventors: Hybrid Extraction of In-text Patent-to-Article Citations. Journal of Economics & Management Strategy. doi:10.1111/jems.12455
