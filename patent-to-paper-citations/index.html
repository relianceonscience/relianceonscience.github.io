Patent-to-Paper Citations

download data > documentation

The datafile containing the citations is pcs_oa_uspto.csv. (Click on DOWNLOAD DATA to retrieve.) Each citation has the applicant/examiner flag, confidence score (1–10), and whether the reference was a) only on the front page, or b) only in the body text, or c) both. We link to papers captured by OpenAlex.

Updated July 2026: coverage now extends through patents granted in 2025, and the previously missing 2021–2022 in-text citations have been restored. USPTO patents, confidence ≥ 4.

If you use the data, please cite these two articles

1. M. Marx & A. Fuegi, “Reliance on Science by Inventors: Hybrid Extraction of In-text Patent-to-Article Citations.” Journal of Economics and Management Strategy. (doi.org/10.1111/jems.12455)
2. M. Marx & A. Fuegi, “Reliance on Science: Worldwide Front-Page Patent Citations to Scientific Articles” (2020), Strategic Management Journal 41(9):1572–1594. (doi.org/10.1002/smj.3145)
