Reliance on Science is a project that provides open source datasets to spur new research regarding innovation and entrepreneurship related to scientific discoveries. All data are provided under a Creative Commons Attribution Non-Commercial 4.0 International License.

Patent-Paper Pairs

Patents often cite papers, but sometimes the patent is the paper. Dozens of scholars have used patent-paper pairs, but there is not yet an open, large-scale dataset. We built a self-plagiarism detector to find pairs across all industries.

get the data >

Startup Patenting

Conventional wisdom holds that new ventures drive innovation. This is hard to verify because the patent data don't indicate which assignees are startups. Prior work has linked assignees to Compustat; we try to link the remaining 95% of assignees to OpenCorporates.
