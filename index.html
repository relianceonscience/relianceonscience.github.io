Open data on innovation, patenting,
commercialization & entrepreneurship

Reliance on Science provides open source datasets to spur new research regarding innovation and entrepreneurship related to scientific discoveries. All resources are licensed under Creative Commons Attribution Non-Commercial 4.0 International, and would not exist without the generous support of the Alfred P. Sloan Foundation.

Patent-to-Paper Citations

Front-page and in-text citations from patents to scientific articles published since 1800. The flagship dataset — considerable computational effort went into its compilation.

Patent-Paper Pairs

Instances where patents directly correspond to published research, identified via self-plagiarism detection methodology across industries.

Startup Patenting

Patent assignees linked to founding years and venture-capital indicators — covering far more of the assignee landscape than public-company matches alone.

Pasteur's Quadrant Researchers

Boundary-spanning scientists (1976–2023) who both publish and patent.
